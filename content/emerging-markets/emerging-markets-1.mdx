## Emerging Markets

There are common requirements for admittance into Intl. Monetary and Trade Organizations (signs of safety for investors)
**Mandatory Standards**
- Financial market transparency and reporting standards
- Banking standards
- Economic reforms
- Legal protection of individual property rights (incl. contract rights)
